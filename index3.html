Transforms :

Transforms are basically two types
  1. 2D Transfroms
  2. 3D Transfroms

2D Transforms

Let's see some of the examples of 2D transform.

rotate():

The rotate() method rotates an element clockwise or counter-clockwise.

3D Transforms

Let's see some of the examples of 3D transform.

rotate():

The rotate() method rotates an element clockwise or counter-clockwise.

rotate() in 3D again involves three properties. They are:
  • rotateX()
  • rotateY()
  • rotateZ()

The rotateX() method rotates an element around it's X-axis.
